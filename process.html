❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary by complexity. A simple website takes 1–2 weeks, while a full SaaS platform can take 6–16 weeks. During our discovery call, we'll give you a precise timeline based on your specific requirements.

How much does a project cost? +

Our pricing is custom based on project requirements. We offer startup-friendly rates with flexible payment models — project-based, retainer, or milestone-based. We believe in complete transparency — no hidden costs ever.

Do you work with international clients? +

Absolutely! We serve clients globally across USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, Australia, and beyond. We offer flexible working hours to accommodate different time zones and use tools like Slack, Zoom, and Notion for seamless remote collaboration.

Will I own the source code and IP? +

Yes, 100%. Upon project completion and final payment, you receive full ownership of all code, designs, and intellectual property. We can also sign an NDA before starting work for your peace of mind.

What happens after the project is launched? +

We offer flexible post-launch support packages. Whether you need ongoing maintenance, feature additions, or performance monitoring, we stay by your side. Most of our clients continue with a monthly retainer for continued growth support.

How do I start a project with NexGenAiTech? +

Simply reach out via our contact form, email, or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a free 30-minute consultation call, understand your requirements, and send you a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing within 24 hours.
